Introduction:

Expert care At life-saving speed:

MMHRC is a pioneer in trauma care, being the first and only institute of Emergency Medicine in South India. Our Accident and Emergency department provides frontline emergency care services at par with international standards. Since time is critical during emergencies, we ensure Zero Waiting Time and accurate treatment to every patient. Our Golden Hour care has been widely applauded as a life-saver.

The department is on its toes 24/7. It is fully-equipped with advanced technological facilities and is stocked with all emergency drugs. We have a success rate of over 70% in reviving cardiac arrest patients. The staff is specially trained to offer urgent and expert care in cases like heart attacks, cardiac arrest symptoms, cerebrovascular accidents, strokes, accidents and trauma, and other emergency situations. The personnel is also trained in resuscitation, first aid for poisoning, ventilator technology and other requisites of Accident and Emergency care.

The motto of MMHRC’s Accident and Emergency department is ‘Care to all patients at par with current international standards of emergency medical services’.Which is why we don’t just treat: we deliver care with compassion, respect, and individual attention.
